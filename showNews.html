China Food Safety Law will be revised since the implementation of the law five year ago
食品安全法实施五年迎首次大修
Beijing Daily by n/a  24 June 2014
A draft revision to the Food Safety Law was submitted for a first reading at the Ninth Session of the 12th NPC Standing Committee. It is the first proposed revision of the law since it took effect on June 1, 2009. The draft attracted attention because the public and the legislators called for guarantee of food safety through the most strict supervision, the most severe punishment and the most serious accountability. The draft increases the number of articles from 104 in the existing law to 159 articles, with the aim of reforming and improving the food safety supervision mechanism in China, establishing strict food safety supervision system to promote social governance on food safety.
23日提交十二届全国人大常委会第九次会议审议的食品安全法修订草案备受关注。用最严格监管、最严厉处罚、最严肃问责，切实保障“舌尖上的安全”，这既是人民群众的呼声，也是立法者的关切。此次修法是食品安全法自2009年6月1日施行以来的首次修改。 草案从现行法律的104条增加到159条，旨在改革完善我国食品安全监管体制，建立严格的食品安全监管制度，推进食品安全社会共治格局。

Pattern for safety of imported dairy products takes shape
进口乳制品安全格局初定
International Business Daily by n/a  24 June 2014
Overseas manufacturers without registration in China are no longer able to export their dairy products to the Chinese mainland market, as new regulations came into effect as of May 1. According to the General Administration of Quality Supervision, Inspection and Quarantine (AQSIQ), China had already certified 1,122 foreign dairy producers, including 41 companies which make infant milk formula. Industry insiders said the new regulations will ensure safety of imported milk powder and cause certain influence on China's industrial pattern. In addition, China also forbade distributing infant milk powder imported in large packages. The imported powder must already be packed into small packages before sold to consumers. The package requirement can ensure the safety of imported milk powder sold in China.
从5月1日起，我国全面实施对进口乳品境外生产企业的注册管理。未经注册的境外企业生产的乳品不得进口。根据国家质检总局公布的信息，首批通过注册的进口乳品生产企业共有1122家乳品境外生产企业，其中婴幼儿配方奶粉生产企业41家。业内人士表示，这一进口乳品新规有利于保障进口奶粉的安全，同时也会对海外奶粉在我国的产业格局造成一定的影响。此外，从5月1日起，我国严禁进口大包装婴幼儿配方乳粉到境内分装，必须已罐装在向消费者出售的最小零售包装中。这样是为了从源头上保证国内销售的进口乳粉符合中国食品安全。
